My background uniquely positions me to deliver hands-on, research-informed bioinformatics education:

- **Software Development**: Lead developer of Datamonkey (datamonkey.org), a widely-used web platform for molecular evolution analysis with thousands of users worldwide, and HIV-TRACE, adopted by the CDC for national HIV surveillance across 59 U.S. jurisdictions.

- **Teaching Experience**: Guest Lecturer for "Genomics in Medicine" at Temple University (2019) and Instructor at the MEGA Workshop (SMBE, Yokohama, 2018), teaching molecular evolutionary genetics analysis to international participants.

- **Research Impact**: 37 peer-reviewed publications including first-author papers on computational methods, with work published in Nature, Cell, and Science Translational Medicine.
